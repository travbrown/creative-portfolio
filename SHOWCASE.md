XPRSV (pronounced "Expressive") is my creative portfolio — a space dedicated to the art of seeing, where I share photography and short films that capture moments, moods, and meaning through a lens. The photography gallery pulls images from Cloudinary and reshuffles them with a Fisher-Yates shuffle on every visit, so no two sessions feel the same. Films are presented in a custom lightbox built over YouTube embeds, while the landing page opens with a cinematic full-screen video that sets the tone before a single word is read.
